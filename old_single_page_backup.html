Our Story

The primal
gathering place
for creators.

HEARTH is a community-powered talent development studio built on one core belief: the stories that change the world are often the ones that never get made — because the people who hold them don't have the resources, access, or community to bring them to life. We exist to change that.

// Brand Essence
Everything we do maps back to the fire:
The Fire
Creative passion and energy at the center of everything
The Fuel
Education, resources, and mentorship that sustain the flame
The Warmth
Community support, belonging, and celebration
The Light
Stories that illuminate, inspire, and create change
The Circle
Inclusive gathering where every voice belongs
Our Vision

A more equitable
media landscape.

To become the defining ecosystem where undiscovered talent thrives, creating a more equitable and empathetic media landscape. We envision a world where our stories spark global conversations and our community is empowered to take meaningful action.

Through innovative and engaging content, we create a platform that inspires, educates, and mobilizes audiences to address critical social issues.

In this future, our media serves as a catalyst for positive change — where every story has the potential to ignite passion, promote dialogue, and enhance community engagement.

Three pillars of impact:

Equitable Access
We build ladders of opportunity for talent that traditional industry structures routinely overlook.
Empathetic Media
Stories that bridge gaps, foster empathy, and build a more connected, compassionate society.
Lasting Impact
We measure success through both impact metrics and revenue — never choosing one over the other.
Core Values

Six principles that guide the fire.

01 —
Lift As We Climb.

Our growth is inextricably linked to the talent we develop. We create ladders of opportunity and celebrate each other's wins.

→ Mentoring without ego, sharing credit openly
02 —
Voice is Value.

Diverse perspectives are not checkboxes — they are our most valuable creative asset and the source of our competitive advantage.

→ Amplify overlooked stories
03 —
Purpose with a Punch.

We reject the false choice between art and commerce. Content can be profoundly meaningful and wildly entertaining.

→ Impact + revenue, always both
04 —
Create Bravely.

True innovation happens outside comfort zones. We provide a safe space to experiment, fail, and learn without blame.

→ Pilot. Iterate. Grow.
05 —
Community is Our Currency.

We are a dialogue, not a monologue. Our audience and talent are active co-creators, not passive consumers.

→ Build with, not for
06 —
Own the Outcome.

We are all founders here. Proactive, resourceful, and relentlessly focused on solutions over excuses.

→ How can I make this better?

About This Program

From spark
to finished draft.

Script Labs is HEARTH's intensive 8-week cohort program for screenwriters developing feature or TV scripts. Professional feedback, peer workshop, and a structured path to a finished or near-finished draft — for writers who have the story but not the infrastructure to complete it.

Script Labs directly addresses the content gap in diverse storytelling by producing finished documents — completed scripts — that writers need to enter every industry conversation. Without a draft, a writer cannot pitch, apply, or submit. Script Labs produces the draft.

How It Works

Week by week

1
Applications open — promoted through The Glow newsletter and social channels
2
CEO selects 12 writers based on concept strength, writing sample, and commitment
3
Cohort notified — onboarding documents, program schedule, and tool access delivered
4
Week 1: Cohort kickoff — in-person Philadelphia for local participants, virtual for national
5
Weeks 2–7: Weekly workshop sessions — craft instruction, peer feedback, professional development
6
Professional script notes delivered to each writer at program midpoint (Week 4)
7
Week 8: Final session — completed or near-completed draft presented to full cohort
8
Post-program survey, alumni records updated, sponsor impact report prepared
Eligibility

Who can apply

  • →Active HEARTH community member
  • →One-page concept or treatment demonstrating project is developed enough for workshop
  • →Writing sample — 5 to 10 pages of original work in any format
